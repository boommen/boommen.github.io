About The WITCH


The WITCH was created to help those people who have difficulty getting out of bed in the morning. Our patented design allows for people to be woken up by their own lights in the morning. Studies have shown that light is a more natural way of waking up than an alarm, which allows for people to get over their drowsiness much more quickly.


How this works


1. Easily install the WITCH on any existing light switch.

2. Add your preferences to our free app, which connects to the WITCH via bluetooth.

3. Never have trouble getting out of bed again with our revolutionary product!
